The Founder

Alberto Pena

What I bring to every client is not a credential. It is twenty years of learning what actually works when the stakes are real and the margin for error is zero.

I have spent my career building and leading high-performing organizations — diagnosing what is broken, designing what needs to change, and holding teams accountable to results that actually matter. That experience does not live in a framework I borrowed from a textbook. It was built in the field, one hard decision at a time.

I founded Futuris Nova because I believe the principles that build elite, high-performing organizations are not exclusive to any sector. Every organization faces the same core challenges: leadership that does not scale, culture that does not perform, strategy that does not execute.

I also believe that artificial intelligence, handled with discipline and directed by strong leaders, is one of the most significant capability multipliers available to organizations right now. I help leaders understand it, direct it, and use it responsibly — as one powerful tool among many, not a replacement for the fundamentals.
