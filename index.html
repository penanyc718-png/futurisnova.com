Our Mission

Why We Exist

The world does not need another consulting firm. It needs a different kind of firm entirely.

For decades, consulting looked the same. Brilliant people in expensive suits flew into your city, handed you a binder, and flew home. The knowledge was real. The access was reserved for those who could afford to pay for someone else to think for them. The outcomes were rarely what the binder promised.

Then artificial intelligence arrived. And most firms did the predictable thing. They added the word to their pitch decks and kept doing what they had always done.

We did not do that.

Futuris Nova LLC was built from day one around a premise that changes everything: human expertise and artificial intelligence are not competitors. They are partners. When you structure a firm around that partnership instead of bolting it on afterward, you unlock something the industry has never seen before.

Our founder carries twenty years of military mastery. The kind of leadership forged before sunrise under real stakes, not theorized in a classroom. That experience does not become obsolete in the age of AI. It becomes the anchor. The judgment, the discipline, the mission focus, the ability to lead under pressure and still make the right call.

What AI brings is everything else. Speed. Scale. Pattern recognition across volumes of data no human team could process alone. The ability to work while you sleep. The capacity to serve an organization of five with the same quality of intelligence as one of five thousand.

Together, that is not a consulting firm. That is a force multiplier.

We believe the organizations that will win in the next decade are the ones that close the gap. The gap between who their leaders are today and who the mission requires them to become. The gap between the decisions they are making and the decisions the data supports. The gap between the culture they have and the culture that actually performs.

We call that gap by its name. And we close it.

This is Futuris Nova. Excellence. Executed.

The Founder

Alberto Pena

Some leaders are made in boardrooms. Alberto Pena was made before sunrise, in the field, leading soldiers through the hardest conditions the mission could produce.

Over twenty years of U.S. Army service, Alberto built a reputation not just for operational excellence, but for developing the people around him. He understood early that the quality of the team determines the quality of the outcome, and that developing people is the most important thing a leader can do.

He founded Futuris Nova LLC on a conviction that the principles that build elite military units belong in every organization. And that artificial intelligence, when treated as a partner rather than a tool, multiplies human leadership rather than replacing it.

Alberto is also a licensed Life and Annuities agent, the creator of MilSume, and the author of Command the Gap. He brings the full range of that experience to every engagement, every stage, and every conversation.

Brooklyn born. Mission driven. Built for what comes next.
