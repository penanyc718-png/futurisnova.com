How We Work

A Four-Phase Approach Built for Results That Hold.

Every engagement follows the same disciplined framework. The specific tools and interventions change based on what the organization needs. The standard of discipline never does. We do not begin designing until we fully understand the problem. We do not leave until the capability is inside the organization.

01
Discover

We start by understanding the real situation — not the presented one. Structured interviews, direct observation, and honest assessment to establish a baseline before any recommendations are made. We do not prescribe before we diagnose.

02
Design

A custom engagement plan built specifically for this organization, this challenge, and these people. No templates borrowed from a different industry. No frameworks adapted for someone else's situation and handed off as custom work.

03
Deliver

Hands-on implementation alongside leadership. Not a document handed off at the door. We work in the organization until the outcomes we committed to are visible, measurable, and real — not just described in a final report.

04
Sustain

Before we close, we build the systems, habits, and accountability structures that allow the organization to maintain and build on the work long after we are no longer in the room. Independence is the goal, not dependency on us.

The Founder

Alberto Pena

What I bring to every client is not a credential. It is twenty years of learning what actually works when the stakes are real, the margin for error is zero, and the people in front of you are counting on you to get it right.

I have spent my career building and leading high-performing organizations — diagnosing what is broken, designing what needs to change, and holding teams accountable to results that actually matter. That experience does not live in a framework I borrowed from a textbook. It was built in the field, one hard decision at a time.

I founded Futuris Nova because the principles that build elite military organizations are not exclusive to any sector. Every organization faces the same core challenges: leadership that does not scale, culture that does not perform, strategy that does not execute. I know how to fix all three.

I also believe that artificial intelligence — handled with discipline and directed by strong leaders — is one of the most significant capability multipliers available right now. I help leaders understand it honestly, direct it with confidence, and govern it responsibly.

"The organizations that win are the ones that close the gap. Between who their leaders are and who the mission requires them to be. Between the strategy on paper and the execution on the ground. That is the work. That is what Futuris Nova does."
