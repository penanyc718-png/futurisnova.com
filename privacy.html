Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Futuris Nova LLC ("Futuris Nova," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of every person who visits futurisnova.com, contacts us directly, or engages with our services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights you have.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers. We collect only what we need to provide our services and communicate with the people who reach out to us.

By using this website or contacting us through any of our channels, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Questions? Contact us directly at [email protected].

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us through the website contact form, by email, or by phone, we collect the information you choose to share — your name, organization, email address, phone number, and any details you provide about your leadership context or inquiry.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit futurisnova.com, certain technical information is collected automatically — including your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This information is used to understand how visitors interact with the site and improve the experience. It is not used to personally identify you without information you have voluntarily provided.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and determine whether Futuris Nova is the right partner for your situation
  • To schedule and conduct discovery conversations, consultations, and engagements
  • To deliver the services you have engaged us for and communicate about your engagement
  • To send relevant information you have requested — resources, proposals, or recommendations
  • To improve the website and better understand how visitors engage with our content
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the legitimate interests of Futuris Nova LLC

We do not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails, sell you products you did not ask about, or share your details with third parties for their marketing purposes.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances: with trusted service providers who help us operate the website and manage communications (including Formspree for contact forms); when required by law or court order; or in connection with a business acquisition or merger, with advance notice to affected individuals.

Data Retention

We retain contact information for individuals who inquire but do not engage for up to 24 months. For clients who engage our services, we retain records of the engagement for a minimum of 7 years to comply with applicable legal and business requirements. When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights and Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at [email protected]. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We will respond to valid requests within 30 days.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including secure transmission protocols (HTTPS), access controls, and working only with service providers who maintain appropriate security standards. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, but we take our obligation seriously.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
