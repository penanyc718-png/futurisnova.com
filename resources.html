The Book

AI-Enabled Military Leadership

How military leaders use artificial intelligence to enhance their people, accelerate decisions, and build competitive advantage.

This is not a technology book. It is a leadership book that takes artificial intelligence seriously as a tool in the hands of someone who knows how to lead. Alberto Pena draws on twenty years of military service to show how the principles that build elite units apply directly to every organization navigating the AI era.

The core argument is simple: AI does not replace leadership. It reveals it. The leaders who thrive in the next decade are the ones who understand how to direct AI with the same clarity, discipline, and purpose they bring to everything else.

Our primary thinking partner for analysis, writing, strategy development, and long-form reasoning.
